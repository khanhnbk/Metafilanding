Use Case

Agriculture (ngoc linh ginseng)

Benefits

For many years, Ngoc Linh Ginseng has been continuously searched by the world's scientists and medical practitioners and discovered more and more special features that are very valuable for human health. The major driven factor for the Ginseng market is increasing demand for health conscious food around the world. Especially after Covid-19 Crisis, this demand is increasing sharply.

Ngoc Linh Ginseng is becoming an industry of high economic value that is branded national products associated with sustainable use of forests based on exploiting local potentials and advantages, increasing income for forest workers, contributing to socio-economic development in the areas where ethnic minorities live, and ensuring national security and defense.
